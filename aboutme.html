ABOUT ME

Hello :D

I am Jagrata Deb and currently I am a school student. I usually spend all of my time only in studying and when I am free, I code.

Aim in life

I want to become a software engineer.

I want to create amazing stuffs and for that I really need to become a Software Engineer or something eqiuvalent.

But, my biggest aim is to be a good human being.

Hobby

Till 2019 my hobby was sketching. But due to several reason I realized that it's not possible to continue it as I could hardly spare some time for it.

I knew that this hobby is no way going to help me. So, I left it and nowadays I rarely sketch anything.

Now, my new and current hobby is to code.

This is my permanent hobby which is going to last forever as I have decided to become a software engineer.

That is what I want in future. Code, Sleep, Eat and Repeat.

My Favourites :D

Coding
Talking to my friends
Noodles
Discord
Spotify

Something Deep...

I am someone who does not like being alone. But, yes in a family of four I really feel extremely lonely as I have no friend at home to talk with.

I am someone knows how to respect others opinion and feeling. I never interfere in other's life.
It's not very tough for me to become someone's comfort zone and so is the reason why even the ones whom I have never met in entire life, also trust me.

I care for everyone. I don't want to hurt anybody and I always try not to do so.
If required, I might harm myself, but I am not ready to harm others. I always want the people around me to stay happy.

I want to enjoy my life. I want a beautiful life and I want to live it with the ones who have got a beautiful heart.

Whenever, I feel lonely or sad or depressed I just write my personal diary. Because there's no one who has got plenty of time to listen to me. Everyone is busy enough with there life.

In life many people come and go. You can't hold back everyone for you. They have their own life, they will move forward and it does not matter whether you exist or not.

This is a actually a world of fakes and snakes and so it's difficult to say that who is trust worthy and who is not.

This is another reason for me being lonely.
However, I have some friends, who are really important to me and I will never ever want to lose them.
